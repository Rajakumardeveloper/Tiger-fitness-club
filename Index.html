About Tiger Fitness

Delhi's Premier Fitness Destination

Tiger Fitness Club is not just a gym - it's a complete fitness ecosystem designed to help you achieve your physical potential. Our advanced facility combines cutting-edge equipment with expert guidance from certified trainers.

Founded by Vikas Dedha (Mr. Delhi & Mr. North India 2022) and Tiger Bhat Guru Ji, our gym embodies the spirit of excellence and determination. We've created a space where beginners and professional athletes alike can thrive.

Our unisex facility ensures a comfortable, motivating environment for everyone. With personalized training programs, nutritional guidance, and a supportive community, we're committed to helping you reach your fitness goals.
